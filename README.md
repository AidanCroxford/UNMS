### Installation Scripts for Ubiquiti Network Management System

We invite you to use our new Ubiquiti Network Management System (beta). You can simply configure, monitor, upgrade, and back up your UBNT devices. Add your routers and switches. You can include your wireless equipment and optical GPON devices as well. And why stop there. You can even manage your client APs with ease. Management of all UBNT devices in a single application.
